AVAILABLE ACCOMMODATIONS:

1. Guest rooms near Vilnius Old Town at Ramybes street (see map)

Guest rooms are in the old part of town, close to the Gate of Dawn (Ausros Vartai) - an ancient city gate in Vilnius that houses a magnificent chapel and a shrine to the Virgin Mary.

They are walking distance to the railway station & international bus station.

A big supermarket Lidl is also nearby.

The guest house is surrounded by flowers, shrubs and trees.

We offer high quality 2- and 4-person rooms. Beds in 4-person rooms are doubles (2 beds per room); 2-person rooms have either two single beds or one double.

Fresh towels are provided in the bathrooms. The kitchen is fully equipped (fridge, microwave, gas range, kettle, dishes, cutlery) and also includes a washing machine.

Free WiFi and parking (cars, motorcycles).

Room prices per day:

  • 2-person room for 2 guests - 35 Euro
    2-person room for 1 guest - 30 Euro

  • 4-person room for 4 guests - 60 Euro
    4-person room for 3 guests - 50 Euro
